Prices

Laminate

Laminate costs can vary widely depending on factors like tree size, location, and complexity. Generally, small trees might cost $150-$400 to remove, while larger ones could range from $500 to $2000 or more. Factors like obstacles, tree condition, and Carpet can also influence the final price.



Hardwood

Hardwood involves various tasks like pruning, trimming, and removal to ensure the health, safety, and aesthetics of trees. It encompasses regular inspections, disease management, and structural support to mitigate risks. Professional arborists employ specialized techniques and equipment, considering factors like species, size, and environmental conditions. Costs vary based on services needed and tree characteristics.



Vinyl

Vinyl costs vary based on factors like tree size, location, and health. On average, expect to pay between $250 to $1,000 per tree. Smaller trees may cost as low as $80, while large or hazardous trees can exceed $1,500. Factors like accessibility and debris removal also influence the final price.



Carpet

Carpet involves the extraction of tree stumps from the ground after a tree has been cut down. It typically requires specialized equipment such as stump grinders or excavators. Costs vary depending on factors like stump size, location, and accessibility, with averages ranging from $75 to $150 per stump.



Tile

Tile costs vary based on factors like stump size, accessibility, and location. On average, prices range from $75 to $1,000 per stump. Smaller stumps might cost around $75 to $300, while larger ones can exceed $1,000. Accessibility challenges or extra services like root removal can increase the expense. Always get multiple quotes for accurate pricing.
